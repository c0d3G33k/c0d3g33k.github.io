Nikhil Mittal

I am Nikhil, a principal security engineer with a proven track record and currently serving at Chargebee. My role is multi-faceted and challenging, encompassing various aspects of platform security. Presently, I am engaged in shielding our staff on a large scale through secure architecture reviews, driving DevSecOps, and diligently working towards eliminating vulnerability classes.

Before assuming my current role, I was deeply immersed in client-side security research. This experience has equipped me with a unique perspective and advanced skills that I now apply in my current role. My contributions to the field include uncovering and reporting security issues in applications, which have been acknowledged by several tech industry leaders.

My expertise has been recognized by major companies such as Google, Microsoft, Mozilla, SANS, Sony, SourceForge, and Intel. This recognition is a testament to my deep understanding of security protocols and my ability to identify and rectify potential security breaches.

Additionally, I have had the privilege of sharing my insights and knowledge at various hacker conferences. I have been a speaker at POC2020, 36c3, and phdays9. These engagements underline my commitment to the security community and my passion for sharing knowledge with fellow professionals and enthusiasts.
