Nikhil Mittal

Nikhil is a principal security engineer at Chargebee. His role is diverse and challenging, covering all aspects of platform security. He is currently focused on ensuring the team's safety through secure architecture reviews, driving DevSecOps, and diligently eliminating vulnerability classes. Before this role, Nikhil was deeply involved in client-side security research, which provided him with a unique perspective and advanced skills that he now applies in his current position. He has also made significant contributions to the field by uncovering and reporting application security issues, earning recognition from major companies like Google, Microsoft, and others for his expertise. Nikhil has also had the honour of sharing his insights and knowledge at various hacker conferences, speaking at Blackhat, POC2020, 36c3, and phdays9. He is passionate about the security community and enjoys sharing knowledge with fellow professionals and enthusiasts.
